# About fable-5.org

## What this site is

fable-5.org is an independent third-party review site maintained by one long-time Claude Pro user.

The site's only goal: **record one ordinary Pro user's real experience using Anthropic Fable 5 in actual work.**

Not official endorsement. Not product recommendations. Not technical whitepapers.

## What this site is not

- **Not an official Anthropic site.** This site has no official relationship, partnership, license, or affiliate arrangement with Anthropic PBC, its subsidiaries, or affiliates.
- **Not a Fable 5 product page.** This site does not sell Fable 5, provide Fable 5 access, or substitute for Fable 5's official documentation.
- **Not a commercial content site.** No ads, no affiliate links, no paid courses, no sponsored posts.
- **Not a neutral review organization.** The author is an actual Fable 5 user. Every review is a record of "using it myself," not a comparison experiment done for someone else.

## Review principles

**Only observations after the run.** No predictions, no forecasts, no echo of official press releases.

**Publish the data.** Every review includes: task description, input prompt, Fable 5 output summary, break location, token consumption.

**Admit mistakes.** If a previous review turns out wrong, correct it immediately and mark the change.

**Accept reports.** If you spot fabrication or data invention, report via Email. Verified reports result in immediate retraction.

## About the domain

The fable-5.org domain was registered on June 12, 2026 — it happened to be unregistered.

This site did not coordinate with Anthropic's trademark team in advance. If Anthropic's trademark team considers this site infringing, this site will cooperate with rebrand — but as of publication, no such notice has been received.

This site maintains: **independent third-party reviews have value for end users.** Even reviews of brand-name products should be allowed to exist, as long as the content is real, the position is independent, and the site doesn't impersonate the official channel.
